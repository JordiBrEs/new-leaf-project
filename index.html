Services

Design

Provide hand drawn sketches for new garden layouts with plants selected to suit your individual tastes and garden site. Photos and plants descriptions provided.

Installation

Installation of hand selected prennials, shrubs, and small trees to fill your garden spaces. Additional soil, amendments and mulching can be provided as indicated.

Maintenance

Regular visits at scheduled intervals throughout the growing sesason to provide weeding, dead heading, pruning, and fertilization to keep your garden beds looking at their best.

Spring and Fall Clean Ups

In the spring, clean up of garden debris with mulch application if desired.
In the fall, prepare beds for winter by cutting back, fertilizing, diving plants as indicated.

Our Story

We are a small team of hard working plant lovers based in Cleveland Heights, OH. Our mission is to create beautiful garden spaces in Cleveland Heights and its surrounding communities. No project is too small. We love to help organize and maintain established gardens. If you are looking for a complete redo, we can provide ideas, individualized sketches, and can install a garden to fill your space with new perennils, shrubs and small trees. Our goal is to provide you with an individualized and unique outdoor garden space that you can call your own.
